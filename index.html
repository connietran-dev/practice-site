Making a Sandwich in an Ordered Way

Here's some practice I was having with styling ordered lists and messing with their line height and max-width.

If you'd like to know how to make a sandwich, just follow these instructions below.

Sandwich Instructions

  1. Get two (2) pieces of bread. (Preferably, pieces from the middle of the loaf. Those end pieces are the worst - unless you like crust.)
  2. Optionally, you can lightly toast your bread with a toaster or pan.
  3. Get the main ingredients you would like on your sandwich, for example, a main protein, lettuce, or tomatoes.
  4. Don't forget to grab some sauces which will enhance your sandwich. Some people like mustard; my favorite is mayo. You can even get fancy and get chiptole mayo.
  5. Place one (1) piece of bread on your plate.
  6. Place your main ingredients on the first piece of bread.
  7. Spread sauce on the other piece of bread.
  8. Place the sauce'd piece of bread on top of your main ingredients, with the sauce side facing down.
  9. You've just made yourself a sandwich!
  10. Bonus points: Garnish with a pickle and chips!
